About the Game


Gatekeeper is a puzzle game where players use logic gates to manipulate circuits and achieve specific outputs.

Each level presents a unique challenge, helping players learn how to design and optimize digital circuits. The game is both educational and engaging, sharpening problem-solving skills while allowing players to climb the leaderboard.

Logic Gates Overview


Logic gates are the fundamental building blocks of digital circuits. They perform Boolean operations on binary inputs (0 and 1) to produce a single output. These gates are essential in computing and digital systems, enabling everything from basic calculations to complex processing.

How to Play


  1. Select a Level – Choose a puzzle to solve.
  2. Build Your Circuit – Drag and drop logic gates, adjust switches, and connect components.
  3. Test Your Design – Run the circuit to see if it meets the objective.
  4. Refine and Solve – Adjust your circuit as needed to succeed.
